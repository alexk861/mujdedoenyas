# DESIGN.md - Müjde Dönyaş

**Role:** You are a high-end web designer creating a "Digital Piano Salon" for  **Mujde Doenyas** .

**Project Goal:** Design a prestigious, non-commercial personal site to showcase Mujde’s piano hobby^^^^^^. **The primary objectives are to increase page views, foster listener engagement through song requests, and host community polls for her next performance**^^^^^^^^.

**Visual Aesthetic:**

* **Theme:** "Gilded Gallery." **Use a sophisticated palette of midnight blue, ivory, and soft gold accents to reflect an elite, private concert feel**^^.
* **Typography:** Elegant, large-scale serif headings for a "classical" atmosphere (e.g., Playfair Display)^^^^^^^^.
* **Feel:** Welcoming yet prestigious; it should feel like an exclusive invitation to a private home performance^^^^^^.

**Site Sections & Interactive Features:**

1. **The Stage (Hero):** A serene, high-definition background image or video loop of Mujde at the piano^^^^^^^^^^^^. Headline: "The Private Salon of Mujde Doenyas."
2. **Musical Journey (Bio):** A warm, personal narrative about her passion for the piano as a hobby, mentioning her love for diverse pieces from Bach to ABBA^^^^^^^^^^.
3. **The Performance Archive:** A curated gallery of her YouTube videos, such as "The Heart Asks Pleasure First" and "Gladiator," categorized by "Classical" and "Contemporary"^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^.
4. **Listener's Choice (Poll):** A prominent, stylized "What should I play next?" section with an interactive voting poll for upcoming songs^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^.
5. **Request a Melody:** A simple, elegant contact form specifically for fans to suggest songs they would like to hear her perform^^^^^^.
6. **Social Connection:** Minimalist links to her YouTube and Instagram to drive traffic and increase view counts^^.

**Technical Design Notes:**

* **Mobile First:** Ensure the voting poll and video gallery are perfectly optimized for mobile viewing^^.
* **No Commercial Elements:** Remove all "Booking," "EPK," or "Management" language. **Replace with "Community" and "Requests"**^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^.
* **Engagement Focused:** Use clear call-to-action buttons like "Vote for the Next Song" or "Suggest a Piece" to encourage interaction^^^^^^^^.
